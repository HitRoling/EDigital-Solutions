What is a legal compliance system?

A legal compliance system helps a company manage the legal and statutory records that support its safety, health, environmental and quality obligations. It gives teams a structured way to control legal appointments, permits, licences, statutory testing, inspection records, expiry dates and evidence needed during audits or client reviews.

In many businesses, legal compliance documents are saved across different folders, emails and paper files. This makes it difficult to know what is current, what has expired and who is responsible for each legal requirement. A system like eSHEQ Comply helps keep these records organized, visible and easier to review.

Why legal compliance control matters

  • Legal appointments must be current, signed and linked to responsible persons.
  • Permits, licences and statutory records often have expiry or review dates.
  • Contractor legal documents must be available before work starts.
  • Audit evidence must be easy to find when compliance is reviewed.
  • Management needs visibility of legal gaps, overdue records and outstanding actions.

Manual legal compliance files vs a digital compliance system

Manual legal compliance files often become difficult to control because appointments, permits, licences and certificates are saved in separate folders. When a record expires, the team may only discover it during an audit, client check or incident investigation.

A digital legal compliance system makes it easier to keep legal records structured and visible. It also supports better follow-up by linking records to responsible persons, departments, contractors, assets, expiry dates and review dates.

Digital legal compliance benefits

  • Less searching through old folders and emails.
  • Clearer control over expiry dates and review dates.
  • Better visibility of missing or expired legal documents.
  • Stronger audit evidence for SHEQ reviews.
  • Improved link between legal records, safety files, assets and training.
