What is eSHEQ Comply?

eSHEQ Comply is a SHEQ compliance management system built to help companies keep important safety, health, environmental and quality records in a clear system structure. It supports the daily work that SHEQ teams need to control, including registers, inspections, audits, risks, contractor records, training files, legal documents and asset information.

The goal of the system is simple: make SHEQ information easier to capture, find, review and follow up. Instead of keeping records in separate folders, spreadsheets and paper files, eSHEQ Comply creates structured areas for each important compliance function.

Main purpose of the system

  • Keep SHEQ records structured and easier to access.
  • Help users manage safety files, audits and inspections.
  • Track risks, controls, findings and corrective actions.
  • Keep expiry dates visible for training, permits, licences and contractor documents.
  • Support better follow-up between managers, coordinators, HR and SHEQ teams.

Why eSHEQ Comply was built

Many companies already have safety, health, environmental and quality records, but those records are often scattered across folders, emails, spreadsheets and physical files. The problem is not always that information does not exist. The problem is usually that the information is difficult to find, update, review and prove when needed.

eSHEQ Comply was built to give teams a better structure. The system helps users work from modules and registers so the records are organized around real SHEQ activities, not just random storage folders.

It helps with control over:

  • Safety file documents and contractor compliance evidence.
  • Inspection checklists and findings that need action.
  • Risk assessment records and review requirements.
  • Internal audits, non-conformances and corrective actions.
  • Employee training, inductions and competency records.
  • Legal appointments, permits, licences and statutory testing.

How the System Works

eSHEQ Comply follows a practical workflow: set up the company structure, add registers, manage records, then review and follow up.

1

Set up company data

Create branches, departments, categories, users, document types, training types and system options.

2

Add registers

Capture suppliers, contractors, assets, employees, documents, fleet and other important records.

3

Conduct activities

Run inspections, audits, risk assessments, safety file reviews and compliance checks inside the system.

4

Track follow-up

Review expiry dates, statuses, findings, action items, evidence, documents and completed records.

System Benefits

The main value of eSHEQ Comply is better structure, better visibility and better follow-up on key compliance information.

For SHEQ teams

SHEQ teams need to manage many moving parts: documents, site requirements, legal records, audits, findings, contractor records, training, inspections and risks. eSHEQ Comply helps place these records into a clear structure.

  • Less searching through separate folders.
  • More consistent registers and record layouts.
  • Better control over expired and reviewed items.
  • Clearer module structure for daily use.
  • Useful pages for audits, inspections and follow-ups.

For management

Management needs visibility. A system helps make it easier to see what exists, what is missing, what needs action and what needs review. This supports better compliance decisions and better preparation for audits and client checks.

  • Better visibility of compliance information.
  • Structured evidence for internal checks.
  • Clearer record ownership and responsibility.
  • Improved tracking of important SHEQ activities.
  • More professional compliance presentation.
