Why eSHEQ Comply Helps

A SHEQ system should make information easier to find, control and review.

Problem: records are scattered

Many companies have safety files, training records, inspections, audit reports and risk assessments saved in different folders. This makes it hard to know what is missing, expired or overdue.

  • Expired documents are difficult to see.
  • Audit findings can be forgotten.
  • Inspection records can go missing.
  • Training renewals are hard to track manually.

Solution: one structured SHEQ system

eSHEQ Comply helps place the main compliance records into clear modules and registers so users can manage information in a more controlled way.

  • Safety files and contractor records.
  • Audits, inspections and risk assessments.
  • Assets, legal records and training matrix.
  • Expiry dates, actions and evidence control.

Common Questions

Simple answers for people searching eSHEQ Comply and SHEQ system information.

What is eSHEQ Comply?

eSHEQ Comply is a SHEQ compliance management system for safety files, audits, inspections, risks, contractors, assets, training and legal compliance records.

What is a SHEQ system?

A SHEQ system helps companies manage safety, health, environmental and quality information in one structured system.

Can eSHEQ Comply help with audits?

Yes. It can support internal audit questions, findings, corrective actions, audit evidence and review records.

Can eSHEQ Comply help with training?

Yes. It can support training records, competency tracking, expiry dates and employee training requirements.
